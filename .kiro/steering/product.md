# Product Overview

## Investza - Wealth Management Platform

Investza is a comprehensive wealth management and investment advisory platform that provides:

- **Portfolio Review Service**: Users can submit their investment portfolios for expert review and personalized recommendations
- **Call Scheduling System**: Multi-step booking flow for consultation calls with investment advisors
- **Wealth Tracker App**: Mobile application for real-time investment tracking and portfolio analysis
- **Educational Content**: Newsletters, events, FAQs, and learning resources about investment strategies
- **Team & Company Information**: About pages showcasing the team, mission, and vision

## Core Features

1. **Portfolio Review Form**: Collects user investment details and sends email notifications to both admin and users
2. **Call Scheduling Flow**: 6-step process including OTP verification, investment range selection, and time slot booking
3. **Content Pages**: Events, newsletters, team profiles, contact forms, and legal pages
4. **Admin Dashboard**: View and manage booking submissions

## Target Audience

Individual investors seeking professional wealth management guidance, portfolio optimization, and transparent investment tracking.
